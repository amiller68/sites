Features

Built for research, not demos.

Every design decision optimizes for reproducibility and transparency — so you can trust the output and explain it to reviewers.

Procedural A* search

Explores combinatorial design space using heuristic pathfinding — not brute force. Finds optimal assemblies fast.

Local-first

Runs entirely on your machine. Your sequences, your registry, your results — never leave your disk.

Explainable results

Every solution comes with a full trace — the heuristic path, scoring rationale, and part-level compatibility breakdown.

Modular constructs

Define targets as composable part graphs. Mix promoters, RBS, CDS, terminators, and custom parts in any topology.

Open formats

Imports and exports standard formats — GenBank, FASTA, SBOL. No vendor lock-in, no proprietary schemas.

Desktop-native

A real desktop application — not a web app in a wrapper. Fast, responsive, and works offline.

How it works

From target to assembly in seconds.

Define what you need, let the search engine find how to build it.

Define your target

Specify the functional architecture of your construct — the parts you need and how they connect. Use the visual editor or describe it in code.

Screenshot: target editor

Search the space

Geney's A* engine traverses candidate assemblies using compatibility heuristics, pruning infeasible paths and scoring viable solutions.

Screenshot: search results

Review & export

Inspect ranked solutions with full explanations. Export as GenBank, FASTA, or SBOL — ready for ordering or further analysis.
